Welcome to the NEOPETRO mandatory safety and operational briefing. Please review the materials below thoroughly before proceeding to the assessment.

Key Safety Protocols:

  • Always use appropriate PPE for specific tasks.
  • Conduct a Pre-Task Safety Talk (PTST) before starting work.
  • Identify and report all near-misses immediately.
  • Maintain a clean and hazard-free workspace at all times.
